Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our boosting services. Can't find your answer? Join our Discord.

How do I place an order?
You place an order by opening a ticket on our Discord server. Our team will guide you through the entire process from there.

Is boosting safe? Will I get banned?
Boosting is completely safe. We have a 0% ban rate across over 1,000 completed boosts with zero bans. Your account is in good hands.
How does the boosting process work?
Depending on your chosen method, we either log onto your account (Solo) or play together with you (Duo) until you reach your desired rank.
What is the difference between Solo and Duo?
Solo means we play on your account — it's quicker, cheaper, and overall the better option. Duo means we play together with you — it takes longer, costs more, and is only available on weekends.
How long does a boost take?
On average, boosts take 1-2 days to complete. However, this can vary depending on the specific boost you ordered (rank range, playlist, method, etc.).
Do you need my account login?
Yes, for Solo boosts we will need your account login information. Your credentials are kept secure and only used for the duration of the boost.
Can I play on my account while being boosted?
No, you cannot play on your account during a Solo boost. Our booster needs uninterrupted access to complete the order efficiently and safely.
What playlists do you boost?
We boost all playlists: 1v1, 2v2, 3v3, 4v4, Rumble, Snow Day, and Tournaments. We also accept custom requests.
Do you boost on all platforms?
Yes, we boost on all platforms — PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch.
What are season rewards?
Season rewards are earned by winning a certain number of games at a rank. After the season ends, you receive special items for reaching that rank — like a boost, banner, or decal. The title is always guaranteed.
